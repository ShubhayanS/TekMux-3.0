About the event

Tekmux, an Aakash Research Labs fest, is back up on its feet and ready to take you out for an electrifying journey across the brain scouring coding competitions, Hackathon and those exhilarating games and events. It is a 2 days long technical fest. This fest has truly been a great experience over the years, while here at the labs we are doing back wrenching work to make this a lot better for you.

Tekmux 1.0, was held on 11th and 12th August, 2017. The lab is a remote one of IIT Bombay under the Department of Electronics and communication, SRM University. Tekmux 2.0, was held on 30th August, 2018 and 1st September, 2018. There was a workshop conducted for the 11th, 12th, and Diploma students and over 200 students had participated in the events.
